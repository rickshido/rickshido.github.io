I am currently an Adjunct Professor at the Department of Computing - Federal University of Technology - Paraná. My research interests are distributed systems and computer networks, mainly in scheduling and resource allocation in grid/cloud/edge computing. I am also involved in health informatics for development of education tools.

After receiving my graduate's degree in IT from the Federal University of Technology (UTFPR), I carried out research on Parallel/Concurrent Computing during my Masters at the State University of Maringá (UEM). And, I have researched on workflow scheduling in clouds during my Ph.D. thesis at the Institute of Mathematics and Computer Sciences - University of São Paulo.
